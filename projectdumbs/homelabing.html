Inspiration and Why

Jeff Geerling and my push into open source and free stuff pushed me into wanting a server and wanting to host and run everything myself, since everything costs money and privacy is my main goal since that is very quickly being lost.

My final straw was seeing the mini rack and NAS of Jeff Geerling and I knew I had to get one, so I ordered parts and had some Pis left over and started with just that — 1 Pi and 1TB to be my NAS running a Tailscale node and that's all. Not a great start but further on I'll explain where I am now.
