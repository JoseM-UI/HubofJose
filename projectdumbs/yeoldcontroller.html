Schematics and Planning

So before I just brute force myself into opening this controller, it'll be nice to see if it was done before, and it has. It's visible on the blog aspect of the controller and I'll be using some of that as reference.

I found a fan-made/community electrical schematic of the controller. All I'm doing is changing the Micro USB to Type-C, so it's pretty standard and nothing too complicated will happen - D+ (Data Plus), D- (Data Minus), GND, and VBUS (5V).
